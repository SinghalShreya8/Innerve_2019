Rules

    Individual Participation


  • Time limit for interjection: 5 minutes 

  • Number of questions per interjector: 1 

  • Time given to answer one interjection: 1 minute

  • Prizes: Worth 10k


  • Participants have to register themselves through the google form given below for any of the given sub-themes i.e. Epics, Trivia and world affairs.

  • The detailed topics of discussion would be mailed to the participants 3 days prior to the event.

  • The situation/question related to their chosen theme would be given to the participants on the day of the event.

  • The participant would be given 2 minutes to justify his stand to the audience and the jury members.

  • The audience can interject the participant after he is done speaking.

** Do not propagate controversial views and be considerate of the emotional/religious beliefs of the audience.
