Rules

    Total team members: 3-5


  • Participants will register in teams of 3-5 people. Each team will be allotted a PC.

  • For the duration of the game, each team will be allowed to carry 1 mobile phone with an app that will be provided to them.

  • The game consists of 5 levels.

  • Each level will be divided into 3 parts.
    1. A coding puzzle will be given to the participants, on solving which theyâ€™ll get the clue to a location within the university campus.
    2. The participants will go to the given location and find a hidden QR code.
    3. On scanning the QR code, a theme based question will be revealed. The answer to this riddle will be the password to unlock the next level.

  • This password will have to be entered on the QR app as well as the respective PC.

  • The team to decode all the levels first wins!

  • Out of the 3-5 members, 1-2 people should always be seated at the PC, solving the coding puzzles. Rest of the team members will go to respective locations to find the hidden QR codes.

  • Team members can interchange their position after each level.

  • The Puzzles include various logical, coding questions, error detection, and Theme based puzzles.

  • In case of a tie, a tie-breaker will take place to determine the winner.
