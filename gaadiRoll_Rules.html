Rules

  • No trial runs will be given.

  • If at any point you touch the robot, you’ll be required to start again.

  • Crossing the starting line before the signal counts for a penalty of 10 seconds.

  • The distance between the two boundaries is 12".

  • The robot can touch the boundary only 3 times and exceeding this will lead to penalty of 20 secs.

  • The dimensions of arena is 72"× 72"

  • The decision of the the Innerve team will be final.
