Space X Rangers

Description:

Day 2 event

No. Of participants: Individual

Registration fee: ₹30 per member

Prizes worth ₹4000

The game is inspired by the SpaceX launch system development program. The game begins with the inception of the gruesome situation which may arise in the future in which the inhabitants of the earth will have to emigrate from Earth to Mars. In the game the player has to emigrate 13 species in three rounds.

Round 1: Stage 1 - Brain teaser round : participants have to decide in what manner species can be emigrated so that they dont harm each other. Stage 2 - Choose your spacecraft : for this the participants will have to crack a code or debug a code as fast as they can in order to get a better option for their spacecraft. In their way the people will face many obstacles , tackling them will provide a protective shield or a weapon like a laser.

Round 2: After the first round the concept of reusable rockets come into place. Now , the rockets already launched to Mars has to be reused to take more species to mars and for that it has to land properly on earth. For this, the player has to face a technical question otherwise the rocket will crash while landing. After safe landing, the player has to take next group of species to mars with enhanced level of difficulty.
