Rules

    NO. OF PARTICIPANTS IN ONE TEAM : min 2 and max 4


  • There will be packets containing some electronic components. These packets will be numbered. The teams have to pick a chit from the bowl and the number written on it will represent their packet.

  • The teams must bring their own Arduino boards and breadboard for the event. Other components will be provided to the team.

  • ROUND 1 : The teams have to formulate an idea for a prototype that can be made using the components present in the packet only. The idea should be creative and has to be presented as a writeup on 1 A4 sheet. The teams will be given 1 hour for this round.

  • Top teams will be selected for Round 2 based on their idea by the judges.

  • ROUND 2 : The teams selected will have to build a prototype of their idea along with a powerpoint presentation. The team will be given 3 hours for this.

  • After 3 hours, the teams have to present their working model and presentation to the judges.

  • Final decision depends on the judges.

    • Judging criteria :

    1.Originality
    2.Impact and real-life application
    3.Creativity and spontaneity
    4.Technical innovation
    5.Presentation
