Rules

  • The weight limit of the robot for this competition is 5 Kg. Robots above this weight will not be considered.

  • The robots are not allowed to use grippers or actuations which are intended to harm the opponent robot.

  • Maximum 2 members per team would be allowed to remain close to the field for operating the robot.

  • A robot can be extended to a dimension of 500mm x 500mm x 500mm maximum (independently) once it crosses the start zone.Robots with dimensions exceeding this dimension will not be allowed to participate further.

  • Any type of mishandling towards the organising committee or the fellow teams is strictly not acceptable. A strict action will be taken against those teams.

  • In case of a tie ,30secs will be given to both teams to score a goal.

  • Penalty will be given to the team if robots go outside the arena or try to destroy the other robot during head on. After 3 such penalties, team will be disqualified.

  • Dimensions for arena - 120*180

  • Goal post outer rectangle - 39*22

  • Goal post inner rectangle - 20.5*15

  • All dimensions are in inches.

  • The decision of the the Innerve team will be final.
