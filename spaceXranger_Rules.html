Rules

    Single Participant Event

    The game begins with the inception of the gruesome situation in which the inhabitants of the earth has to emigrate from Earth to Mars.


  • Each participant will be allotted a PC.

  • For the duration of the game, no external help will be allowed.

  • The game consists of 3 levels.

    1. For the rocket to get launched, the player will have to ensure that the pre-described conditions are fulfilled.

    2. Once the rocket is launched, the player will face various obstacles throughout the journey to mars.

    3. Once the first round to mars is completed successfully,the concept of reusable rockets of Space-X comes into picture in which the rocket has to be re-launched back to earth so that it can take the rest of the species to mars. For this, the player has to solve certain questions for the safe landing on earth.
