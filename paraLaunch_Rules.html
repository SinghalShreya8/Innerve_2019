Rules

  • No outside material is allowed.

  • If the parachute gets stuck in rails or other obstacles during the flight, the team will not be given second chance.

  • The teams can take a second chance if their parachute lands on the ground and the average time will be considered in this case.

  • In round 1 parachutes will be deployed from first floor and in second round they will be deployed from 2nd floor.

  • The decision of the the Innerve team will be final.

  • Participants are supposed to carry their own basic stationary.
