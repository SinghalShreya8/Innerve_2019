Rules

  • Teams must be present half hour before the starting of the event.

  • Dimensions - 30 * 15 * 20 cm

  • Any kind of cheating or plagiarism won't be entertained.

  • A team can have a maximum of 4 participants out of which only 2 can accompany the robot till the track area.

  • A maximum of 12 minutes (Includes your time of programming and track completion) is allowed for a robot to complete the course. Those not completing in the allotted time will be disqualified.

  • Time is measured from the time robot crosses the starting point till its forward most wheel,track or leg touches or crosses the finishing line.

  • There will be 3 pauses or breakdown allowed for robots on the way to the finish line.

  • Dimension of arena -72"x72" ,Width of track- 1"

  • In case the teams reach finish point at the same time the efficiency of the robot would be considered for judgement.

  • Robots once crossed the starting line must remain fully autonomous or it will be disqualified.

  • Any robot that loses the line course must reacquire the line at the point it was lost only once or second attempt will start from starting point.

  • Any robot that loses the line course and fails to reacquire it will be allowed a single reattempt. The robot must start the course again from the beginning. If it loses the line course on its second attempt it will be disqualified.

  • The decision of innerve team will be final.
