Rules

  • Time limit- 3hrs (2hrs making- 1hr testing).

  • Arena will be open ground.

  • .Group of maximum 3 people.

  • Single round competition, 3 chances will be given out of which best time and distance will be noted.

  • Participants have to hand launch their gliders.

  • The decision of the the Innerve team will be final.

  • Participants are supposed to carry their own basic stationary.
